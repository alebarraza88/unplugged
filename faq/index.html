Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

All our properties are close to a major airport. So, whether you’re in a major town or not, you won’t be stuck in transit. And our state of the art shuttle drivers will take care of getting you there and back.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

Anyone would like to create an event that your team will remember and set a foundation for what the company culture will be like for the rest of the new hires to follow!

What size teams can you accomodate?

No matter how big the group is, 10 to 200+, everyone can have their own room or share doubles.

Is there really no wifi available?

The retreat is designed to UNPLUG and get teams to have fun and connect doing in-person activities! However connection isn't all about the workplace so we do have a designated computer room for those who must wifi during the retreat!
